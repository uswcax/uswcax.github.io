学社章程

1.尊重以社团全体大会为最高领导机构。 2.坚持正常的三观且维护世界的公共道德。 3.每月开一次全体会议，由社长和书记、议长商讨内容和时间，其他成员可以上交主题，会议上多多发言，表达自己的见解，会议所做出的裁决有社团最高权限。(全部社员都要参加，倘若没时间则需要提前汇报工作和申请资料) 4.积极讨论学术问题，共同学习。 5.忠于职守，每个人要做好自己的工作。 6.以和平和科学为最终发展目的。 7.对于所有解释权由社团大会保留。

1. Respect the supreme leading body of the general assembly of the society. 2. Adhere to the normal three views and maintain the public morality of the world. 3. A plenary meeting is held once a month. The president and the secretary and the chairperson discuss the content and time. Other members can submit the subject, speak a lot at the meeting, and express their opinions. The decisions made by the meeting have the highest authority of the community. (All members must participate, if there is no time, you need to report work and application materials in advance) 4. Actively discuss academic issues and learn together. 5. Loyalty to duty, everyone should do their job well. 6. Take peace and science as the ultimate development goal. 7. All interpretation rights are reserved by the association meeting.

学社条例

System

关于学社的管理制度，将延续之前党的管理制度，除了名称和少量制度将基本不变 元首一职将撤去，由社长代替其，而社长的职务也将每三次学社大会重新选举一次。 现在学社大会已经进行到第四次。

Regarding the management system of the academy, the previous party management system will be continued, except for the name and a few systems will remain basically unchanged The post of head of state will be removed and replaced by the president, and the post of president will also be re-elected every three academic conferences. Now the academic society conference has been held for the fourth time.
